Zambia

Zambia is a landlocked country in southern Africa known for its rich culture, diverse wildlife, and natural beauty. It is home to the famous Victoria Falls, one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, as well as vast national parks, rivers, and friendly communities. Zambia continues to grow in tourism, mining, and agriculture, making it a vibrant and welcoming nation.
