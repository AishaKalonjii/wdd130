Whitewater Rafting Adventures

Our mission is to provide safe, exciting, and memorable whitewater rafting experiences for adventurers of all skill levels. We believe in the transformative power of nature and adventure.

History

Founded in 1995 by river enthusiasts John and Sarah Waters, our company started with just two rafts and a passion for sharing the thrill of whitewater. Over the years, we've grown to become one of the region's most trusted rafting outfitters, introducing thousands to the joys of river adventure while maintaining our commitment to safety and environmental stewardship.
